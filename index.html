Jinhui Liu

I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Science at Columbia University, advised by Prof. Zhou Yu . My research interests center around Natural Language Processing, AI agent, and Text-to-SQL agent systems. My goal is to develop practical NLP-powered systems that can be broadly applied to assist human-AI collaboration.

Research Interests

My primary research interests are LLM-based Agentic systems. My current research focuses on the LLM-based Text-to-SQL agent in the Business Intelligence(BI) scenario. Specifically, I am interested in designing agentic Text-to-SQL systems that can perform multi-step analytical reasoning and building an automated evaluation framework to evaluate BI agent performance on private enterprise databases.
